Rules and Regulations

ELIGIBILITY

  • This Competition is open, free of registration or other kind of fee.
  • All Contestants must have access to the Internet, a valid postal address, and a valid email address in order to enter.
  • Finalists will be requested to provide official documents as proof of age and identity.
  • GDG Jalandhar, IIT Mandi and the organizing team reserves the right to verify eligibility and to adjudicate on any dispute at any time.
  • The Competition is void where prohibited by law.
  • All communications between Apptitude Organizers and Contestants, including but not limited to the Competition website content and email communications, will be in English.

HOW TO ENTER

  • All Contestants must register and provide the required personal information at the Competition Website, located at: www.apptitude.co.in
  • Contestants can participate either single or in a team of upto four members.
  • Contestants may only register for the Competition with one valid email address. Contestants attempting to compete with multiple email addresses will be disqualified.

COMPETITION STRUCTURE

  • First (Idea Submission) Round : The Competition will start with an Idea Submission Round on March 5, 2015 and ends on March 15, 2015. All registered teams will have to describe their ideas for mobile applications. Contestants can submit more than one idea during this round. Teams which will pass the evaluation for first round, will be eligible to proceed to the next round.
  • Second (Application Submission) Round : The qualifiers of the first round will be informed by e-mail, within a day after the deadline of the first round. During the second Round, the qualifiers will have to confirm within a day after the announcement (i) their participation to the second round (ii) which one of their qualified ideas choose to proceed with the implementation. The contestant should submit their completed app, along with a YouTube video demonstration of the app. Last day for submission of app is March 28, 2015. The list of selected applications will be released on 30 th March. The qualifiers will be informed via e- mail.
  • App Expo : Selected applications will be called for the final presentation either in person at the IIT Mandi campus during the expo or virtually via Google Hangout on April 4, 2015 . ENVIRONMENT. Contestants may use any programming language to implement their proposed applications, using any development environment they wish. The only restriction, is the final application to be a native Android application.

TOPICS

Contestants must build an app targeting any one of the following :-

  • Education system in India
  • Indian tourism
  • Health and fitness
  • Social cause

Judging and Scoring

  • First Round: The scoring criteria of this round are: Originality, Usefulness and Feasibility of the proposed mobile application.
  • Second Round: The scoring criteria of the second round are: Idea fulfillment, Design, User Experience, Compatibility and Accessibility of the implemented mobile application.
  • App Expo: The finalist teams will be evaluated by our Judges. Peer Evaluation would also be given a weightage. The final criteria will be announced soon.

ANNOUNCEMENT OF WINNERS AND PRIZES

Winners will be notified by e-mail within a day after the deadline of the second round.
